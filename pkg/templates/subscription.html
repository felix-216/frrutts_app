HOW FRRUTTS WORKS

WE SPEAK TO GROWERS

Each week we call up growers and ask what they’ve picked and what’s going spare. Fruit and veg can be at risk of going to waste for all kinds of reasons – it could be “too big”, “too odd” or just “too many”.

DELIVER OVERNIGHT

Once you’ve made your swaps and chosen your add-ons, we’ll pack everything up and deliver overnight to keep emissions low.

MAKE YOUR BOX YOUR OWN

Our grower-led way of working means every week is different at Oddbox. Curvy cucumbers one week; ginormous spuds the next. It’s all part of the fun.

PICK A BOX

Small, medium or large? Fruit, veg or both? Whichever box you choose, you’ll be fighting food waste and supporting growers.

MAKE YOUR SWAPS

We’ll email you when the rescue list is ready – then you can swap each item in your box for something you prefer. Apples for onions? Plums for pineapple? It’s up to you.

CHOOSE YOUR ADD-ONS

Look out for our weekly ‘add-ons’ email for extra-special, last-minute rescues that you can add to your box. We also sometimes offer special treats and freebies from our partners.

GET STARTED

KEEP ONTOP OF YOUR BOXES

SKIP A WEEK

Fruit bowl still full? Manage your box via your account and use the ‘Skip Box’ button to skip for up to four weeks.

SWAP YOUR BOX

Whether you need a bigger box, or just fancy more fruit, you can switch to a different box type whenever you like.

STOP RESCUING

If you decide to leave Frrutts, head to your account and click cancel. You're welcome back at any time.

NEED HELP?

Not sure which box to choose? Or want to skip, swap or stop? You’ll also find answers to all our most-asked questions in our FAQs. Or contact our Customer Happiness Team.
