FIGHTING FOOD WASTAGE,

ONE CURVY CUCUMBER

AT A TIME.

As much as 40% of food produced globally is wasted.

And when food is wasted, all the water and energy used to grow it is at risk of being lost too.

Most people don't realise it, but food wastage is a climate issue- and one of the most urgent ones at that.

Food wastage is responsible for ~10% of all greenhouse emissions.

The environmental impact of food wastage is 284x bigger than plastic.

As much as 2.5 billion tonnes of food are wasted globally each year.

But it doesn’t have to be this way... ‘reducing food waste’ is the #1 solution to solve climate change.

So what are we waiting for?

GETTING TO THE ROOTS OF THE PROBLEM.

A lot of what we do focuses on tackling food waste on farms. Around 1.2 billion tonnes of fruit and veg are lost here annually.

25% of UK food waste happens on farms – more than retail, manufacturing, and hospitality combined.

FRUIT AND VEG WASTE

"TOO ODD"

Fruit and veg that don’t meet strict cosmetic standards.

"TOO MANY"

Surplus crops from weather or order cancellations.

HOW ARE WE HELPING?

BY RESCUING THE "TOO-ODD" & "TOO MANY"

BY BEING FLEXIBLE & RELIABLE

We’re a dependable secondary market, ready for last-minute rescues.
